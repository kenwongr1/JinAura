The Science科学原理

Why Molecular Hydrogen? 为什么选择分子氢？

A 2007 landmark study in Nature Medicine established H₂ as a highly selective antioxidant — igniting 1,600+ subsequent peer-reviewed studies across 170+ research areas. 2007年《自然医学》里程碑式研究证实H₂具有高度选择性的抗氧化特性，由此引发超过1,600项涵盖170+研究领域的同行评审研究。

Selective Antioxidant选择性抗氧化

H₂ selectively neutralises harmful free radicals (•OH & ONOO⁻) while preserving beneficial reactive oxygen species — a more targeted approach than conventional antioxidants.H₂选择性中和有害自由基（•OH和ONOO⁻），同时保留有益活性氧，相较传统抗氧化剂更具针对性。

Molecular Bioavailability分子生物利用率

H₂ is an exceptionally small molecule capable of passing through biological membranes, delivering antioxidant support at the cellular level.H₂是极小的分子，能够穿越生物膜，在细胞层面提供抗氧化支持。

Research Frontiers前沿科学探索

Emerging research explores how H₂ may interact with the body's natural protective pathways, with scientists studying its potential role in supporting overall cellular health.新兴研究探索H₂与人体自然保护通路的相互作用，科学家正研究其支持整体细胞健康的潜在作用。

"Hydrogen selectively neutralises harmful free radicals, acting as a potent antioxidant."

「氢气选择性中和有害自由基，发挥强效抗氧化作用。」

— Nature Medicine, 2007 · Ohsawa et al.
