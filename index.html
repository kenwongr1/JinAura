The Science科学原理

Why Molecular Hydrogen? 为什么选择分子氢？

A 2007 landmark study in Nature Medicine established H₂ as a selective therapeutic antioxidant — igniting 1,600+ subsequent peer-reviewed studies across 170+ disease models. 2007年《自然医学》里程碑式研究证实H₂是选择性治疗性抗氧化剂，由此引发超过1,600项涵盖170+疾病模型的同行评审研究。

Selective Antioxidant选择性抗氧化

H₂ selectively neutralises the most cytotoxic free radicals (•OH & ONOO⁻) while preserving beneficial reactive oxygen species — unlike conventional antioxidants that indiscriminately quench all ROS.H₂选择性中和最具细胞毒性的自由基（•OH和ONOO⁻），同时保留有益活性氧，这是传统抗氧化剂无法实现的精准保护。

Deep Cell Penetration深层细胞渗透

As the smallest molecule known, H₂ effortlessly crosses all biological membranes — including the blood-brain barrier — delivering antioxidant protection at the deepest cellular level.H₂是已知最小的分子，能毫不费力地穿越所有生物膜，包括血脑屏障，在最深层的细胞层面提供抗氧化保护。

Gene Expression基因表达调节

Emerging research shows H₂ modulates gene expression, activating protective pathways like Nrf2 while suppressing pro-inflammatory NF-κB signalling — a truly systemic health benefit.新兴研究表明H₂调节基因表达，激活Nrf2等保护通路，同时抑制促炎NF-κB信号传导，提供真正的系统性健康益处。

"Hydrogen acts as a therapeutic antioxidant by selectively reducing cytotoxic oxygen radicals."

「氢气通过选择性还原细胞毒性氧自由基，发挥治疗性抗氧化作用。」

— Nature Medicine, 2007 · Ohsawa et al.

Supports mitochondrial function and ATP production, resulting in improved energy levels, reduced fatigue, and enhanced physical performance and endurance.支持线粒体功能和ATP产生，提升能量水平，减少疲劳，增强体能表现与耐力。

Brain & Cognitive Health大脑与认知健康

Cardiovascular Support心血管健康
